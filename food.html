Indonesian Balinese Dishes

Ayam Betutu

A popular Indonesian dish made with grilled chicken, served with a spicy peanut sauce and steamed rice. The peanut sauce is made with a mixture of peanuts, chili peppers, garlic, ginger, and other spices, giving it a rich and savory flavor. The dish is often served with a side of mixed vegetables, such as bean sprouts, cabbage, and carrots, and a slice of fried tempeh or tofu. Ayam betutu is a staple dish in Bali and is often served at special occasions and ceremonies.

Nasi Goreng

Indonesia's famous fried rice, often served with a fried egg on top and a side of crackers.

Satay

Grilled skewered meat served with a rich peanut sauce, a favorite street food across Indonesia.

Gado-Gado

A healthy salad of boiled vegetables, tofu, and tempeh, topped with a savory peanut sauce.
