Indonesian Cuisine

Rendang

A spicy meat dish originating from the Minangkabau region of West Sumatra, often called the world's most delicious food.

Nasi Goreng

Indonesia's famous fried rice, often served with a fried egg on top and a side of crackers.

Satay

Grilled skewered meat served with a rich peanut sauce, a favorite street food across Indonesia.

Gado-Gado

A healthy salad of boiled vegetables, tofu, and tempeh, topped with a savory peanut sauce.
